Our Story

A Legacy of Luxury and Hospitality Since 1995

Our Heritage

Established in 1995, Naransuitesinternational hotel limited has been a cornerstone of luxury hospitality in the heart of the city. What began as a boutique riverside property has evolved into one of the region's most prestigious hotels, while maintaining our commitment to personalized service and authentic local experiences.

Over the years, we have welcomed travelers from around the world, offering them an oasis of elegance and tranquility along the scenic riverbanks. Our dedication to excellence has earned us numerous accolades, making us a preferred choice for discerning guests, business travelers, and vacationers seeking an exceptional stay.

Despite our growth and modernization, we have remained true to our roots—honoring the local culture, supporting the community, and preserving the natural beauty of our surroundings. Our architecture reflects a seamless blend of contemporary design and traditional charm, creating a timeless atmosphere that resonates with both history and innovation.

Today, NaransuitesinternationalHotel Limited continues to redefine hospitality by combining world-class amenities, award-winning dining, and bespoke experiences tailored to each guest. Whether you seek relaxation, adventure, or a taste of the region’s rich heritage, we invite you to be part of our ever-evolving legacy.

Our Mission

Our mission at NaransuitesinternationalHotel is to create extraordinary experiences that seamlessly blend luxury with authentic local culture, offering our guests an unparalleled stay filled with warmth, elegance, and unforgettable moments. We strive to provide world-class hospitality that goes beyond expectations, ensuring every visitor enjoys the perfect harmony of comfort, sophistication, and personalized service. Rooted in a deep appreciation for our surroundings, we are committed to setting new standards in sustainable hospitality. Our eco-conscious practices focus on minimizing our environmental footprint while preserving the natural beauty and rich heritage of our riverside location. Through responsible tourism, ethical sourcing, and community engagement, we aim to make a positive impact on both our guests and the local community. At NaransuitesinternationalHotel, excellence is not just a goal—it is our way of life. Whether it’s through our thoughtfully curated accommodations, exquisite dining experiences, or heartfelt service, we are dedicated to creating lasting memories that inspire and uplift every guest who walks through our doors.

Community Engagement

At Naransuites International Hotel, we are committed to supporting our community. Through a range of initiatives and collaborations, we actively promote local development and work towards environmental conservation.

Our commitment extends to supporting local businesses, sourcing fresh ingredients from nearby farmers, and promoting sustainable tourism that benefits both visitors and the local economy. We collaborate with nonprofit organizations to fund educational programs, provide employment opportunities, and enhance the well-being of underserved communities.

Additionally, we take pride in our eco-friendly practices, such as reducing plastic waste, implementing energy-efficient systems, and protecting the natural beauty of the riverfront. By organizing regular clean-up drives, tree-planting initiatives, and wildlife conservation programs, we ensure that future generations can continue to enjoy the breathtaking landscapes that surround us.

At Naransuites International Hotel, luxury is paired with responsibility, and we warmly welcome our guests to join us on our path toward a more sustainable and compassionate future.
